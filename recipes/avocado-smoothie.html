Creamy Avocado Smoothie

About

  • Total: 5 mins
  • Servings: 2

This is a healthy way to start your morning with a no added sugar avocado smoothie.
This is a simple recipie that requires a blender and a few items you can probably find
in your kitchen.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • Â½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

    Place milk, avocado, yogurt, and honey into a blender; add ice cubes and blend until smooth (40-50s).

Enjoy!
